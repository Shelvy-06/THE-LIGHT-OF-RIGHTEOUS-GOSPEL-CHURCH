ABOUT US

We are a multi-generational church, built on the faith of our founding members. Our mission is to see individuals of all ages, races, and economic statuses daily cultivating their relationship with God.

MINISTRIES

Nursery: Ages 0-4
Our nursery is open and fully staffed on Sunday mornings. We are ready to not just
supervise your children, but to engage them in music and age-appropriate learning activities.

‚Äã LRGC Kids: Ages 5-12
When children enter the Education Building with their family, they are welcomed into a
secure and loving environment. Each child is issued a name tag with a unique code that
matches their parents pick-up tag. They begin with breakfast and free-play until we begin our
Song list, followed by either grade-level classes, or Kid's Church, an all-age experience
designed to grow their faith in God and knowledge of the Scripture.




Fusion Youth: Ages 12-30
Our Youth ministry, led by Evanglist Evang Alalade Joshua, exists to take young adults even deeper in their
relationship with God during this transitional period of their lives. This involves a Sunday
morning class setting, as well as a Friday night service just for them where prayer, study, and
Godly teaching take priority.




Harvest Ministry: All Ages
Once a month, the Harvest Ministry hosts a food pantry free of charge to any community
members in need. This event takes place on the second Saturday of the month from 4-6pm.
As well as providing families with food items, team members will discuss family needs and
pray with each person that attends. The Harvest Ministry is led by Decon Adeoba Temitope.




Men's Ministry: All Ages
The men's ministry at CAC places emphasis on men encouraging and growing one another's
faith. They also like to have cookouts. The men's ministry is led by Pastor A.Adesina.
